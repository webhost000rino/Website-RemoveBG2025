Frequently Asked Questions

Is ImagePro really free?

Yes, ImagePro is 100% free to use. All features, including AI background removal, enhancement, compression, and cropping, are available at no cost with no usage limits.

Are there any hidden fees or subscriptions?

No. ImagePro is fully free, with no subscriptions or hidden fees. We believe in making professional image editing accessible to everyone.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All image processing happens in your browser, so your images are never uploaded to any server. Your data stays private and secure.

What image formats are supported?

ImagePro supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats, with a maximum file size of 10MB. Outputs are saved as PNG to preserve transparency.

Can I use ImagePro on mobile devices?

Yes! ImagePro is responsive and supports touch gestures, such as swiping up to upload images, making it easy to use on phones and tablets.
